About ASS Construction

ASS Construction was founded by three partners: Alexis, Sophia, and Shajan. Together, we combine planning, technical skills, and on-site experience to handle a wide range of construction needs.

Alexis – Operations Lead

Focuses on day-to-day site operations, safety, and workforce coordination to keep every project moving smoothly.

Sophia – Design & Planning

Works on drawings, layouts, and planning so that projects are clear, efficient, and ready to build.

Shajan – Technical & Offshore Support

Handles technical details, offshore support, and communication with clients to make sure everything is understood and delivered.
